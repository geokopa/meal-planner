💡 Your Rules — Simple & Clear

🍞 Bread Rule

1–2 slices of sourdough/rye OR 1 tortilla per day — always at breakfast. Never at dinner. This single rule has the biggest impact on liver fat and your HbA1c creeping up.

🐟 Salmon Rule

Aim for salmon 3–4x this week. It's your single best food: omega-3 lowers liver fat, vitamin D supports your prescription, B12 keeps your trending-down levels stable.

🌰 Walnut Rule

10–14 walnuts daily. Keep a bag at your desk. Multiple clinical trials show walnuts specifically reduce ALT and AST in NAFLD patients. This is low-effort, high-impact.

☕ Your Drinks

Espresso is fine — 1–2/day, no sugar. Green tea is actively beneficial for your liver (2–3 cups/day is ideal). Water 8–10 cups. Avoid all juice and soda.

🌙 Night Craving Strategy

Don't fight the craving — have your planned snack ready at 9pm before the urge peaks. Greek yogurt + berries or dark chocolate + walnuts + green tea are your go-to weapons.

🚫 Hard Avoids

Zero alcohol while ALT/AST elevated. No white bread or sugary drinks. Limit sour cream to 1 tbsp max. Feta is fine — it's low-fat cheese. Rice is okay in ½ cup portions.

🏃 Movement

30 min brisk walk 5x/week reduces liver fat independently of diet. A walk after dinner is especially powerful for blood sugar and belly fat. Your HbA1c will thank you.

📅 Sunday Prep

Cook chicken + quinoa in bulk on Sunday. Hard-boil 4 eggs. Cut carrots and cucumber. Portion yogurt. Having food ready removes the moment of weakness that leads to bread at night.
